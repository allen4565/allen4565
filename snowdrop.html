Snowdrop(Galanthus)
  • Family: Amaryllidaceae

  • Genus: Galanthus

  • Native: Europe & Middle East

  • Morphology: Herb


Description

            Galanthus is a genus involving about 20 speciesof bulbous perennial
    herbaceous plants. All of these species have many common features:
    pendulous white flower, six perianths in two whorls, two leaves, etc.

            Snowdrop can survive an adverse condition with its bulb underground.
    Most of the species bloom in early spring, some in autumn. Their leaves die
    back weeks after the flower faded. The plant is toxic with an active agglutinin
    - GNA(Galanthus nivalis agglutinin).
