Rosemary(Rosmarinus officinalis)


  • Family : Lamiaceae
  • Genus : Rosmarinus
  • Native: Mediterranean & Western Asia
  • Morphology : Shrub
                          Perennial
Discription

    Rosemary is an aromatic shrub with
slender, needle-like leaf. It can
withstand droughts, surviving a severe
lack of water for lengthy periods.
The seeds are often difficult to start, with a low germination rate and relatively slow growth, but the plant can live as long as 30 years.

    The upright forms can reach 1.5 m tall. The leaves are evergreen, 2 to 4 cm long, green above, and white below, with dense, short hair.The plant flowers in spring and summer in temperate climates, but the plants can be in constant bloom in warm climates; flowers are white, pink, purple or deep blue.
